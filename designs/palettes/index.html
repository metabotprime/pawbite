How to decide

1.

Open all 5 in new tabs. Look at the hero first — that's the page that does 80% of the brand work.

2.

Compare each against the current site at pawbite.vercel.app. Which one looks more like a brand you'd buy from?

3.

Tell Claude the option number. The chosen palette gets applied to the live site (tailwind config + globals.css + a few component tweaks).

Note: These are simplified mockups — hero + stat band + product showcase + footer. The real homepage has 13 sections. Once you pick a palette, all 105+ pages on the live site inherit the new colors automatically (they use Tailwind tokens, not hardcoded hex).
